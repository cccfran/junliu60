Let's celebrate Jun!

We are coming together to celebrate Jun Liu’s remarkable contributions to Data Science, Statistical Learning, and the broader academic community since 1988. From pioneering research to mentoring nearly 80 PhD students and postdoctoral fellows, Jun has profoundly shaped modern statistics across institutions and continents. His influential work in computational biology has also forged vital connections between statistics and biomedical research, deepening our understanding of complex biological systems.

This two-day symposium will take place in Boston, beginning the afternoon of Sunday, July 27, and continuing through the afternoon of Monday, July 28, 2025. The program will include academic sessions and discussions on recent developments in Data Science, AI, and related areas. Planning is still underway, and no speaker invitations have been issued yet. We warmly welcome your ideas and participation as we shape the agenda. Special thanks to Jun’s former students, friends, and Two Sigma for their generous support of this event, and to Harvard Department of Statistics for graciously hosting.
